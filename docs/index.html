Blue Planet Ventures Inc.

Building conviction, one step at a time.

Incorporated in Wyoming in June 2024, Blue Planet Ventures was founded to pursue a different kind of investing — one rooted in discipline, transparency, and long-term value.

Our current focus includes:

  • Proprietary equities trading
  • Establishing an institutional Bitcoin treasury
  • Exploring future mining operations in underutilized regions (including Wyoming)
  • Researching AI-enhanced tools for signal generation and capital deployment

We don’t hold Bitcoin in the company treasury yet — but that’s where we’re headed. Because we believe capital should reflect reality — not speculation.

Blue Planet Ventures is early, but intentional. Quiet, but principled. And built with a long view.
